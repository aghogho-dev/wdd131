Crystal Restaurant

Site Overview

The name of the website is crystal-restuarant.org. It is the digital arm of the crystal restaurant business. Everything from reservations and orders are taken from the website

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to serve as the digital channel of Crystal Restaurant to get to a larger audience

Scenario

Would I be able to make my orders from the website?

Can I talk to a human if my orders are not delivered correctly?

Is using the website more or less convenient that going to the restaurant physically?

Audience

The primary audience are diners who wants to have their orders delivered or make a reservation.
